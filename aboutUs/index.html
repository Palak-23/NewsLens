About Us

Newslens! Combating Fake News

Welcome to Newslens, where innovation meets responsibility. At the heart of our venture is a shared commitment to combat misinformation with the use of Machine Learning algorithms. Founded as a minor project in our college journey, we're a team of dedicated students passionate about establishing a digital environment founded on truth and authenticity. Our mission is both simple and profound: to empower individuals with a dependable tool that harnesses the capabilities of Machine Learning to detect and counteract fake news. Through thorough research and the implementation of advanced algorithms, we are committed to delivering a user-friendly platform that encourages media literacy and responsible information consumption.

Together, let's navigate the digital realm with confidence, recognizing that truth is the cornerstone of our commitment!
